Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

Welcome to NVIDIA NIM (the "Application"). Your privacy is fundamental to our mission of providing a powerful yet secure AI experience. This Privacy Policy details how we handle your information, our commitment to data transparency, and our use of local technologies.

Our Core Philosophy: We prioritize your privacy by keeping your conversation history stored locally on your own device. Your messages are processed in real-time to generate responses and are not used for profile-building or advertising.

1. Information We Collect

During your use of the Application, we collect the following types of information:

  • Message Content: The text and files you input into the chat are transmitted to our servers to generate responses via AI models.
  • Technical Identifiers: We use an anonymous Session ID to maintain conversation context during a live session.
  • Device Information: Basic technical logs (IP address, browser type) may be processed by our infrastructure for security, rate-limiting, and server health monitoring.

2. How Your Data is Used

Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Response Generation: To provide accurate AI-driven answers to your queries.
  • System Improvement: Anonymous logs may be reviewed to fix bugs, optimize performance, and harden security.
  • Safety & Compliance: To prevent abuse, avoid toxic content generation, and comply with legal requirements.

3. Local Storage Policy (Cookie Policy)

NVIDIA NIM does not use traditional tracking cookies. Instead, we use Browser Local Storage to provide a seamless user experience. This information remains on your device and is not shared with us for tracking purposes.

Feature Type Purpose
History Consent Local Storage Stores your choice regarding the long-term saving of chat history.
Chat History Local Storage Stores your past conversations locally so you can resume them later.
Active Session Local Storage Maintains your current connection state and conversation context.

Note: You can delete this data at any time by clearing your browser cache or using the "Clear History" function in settings.

4. Third-Party Integration

To provide high-quality intelligence, we integrate with industry-leading AI providers. When you send a message, it is processed by one of the following:

  • NVIDIA NIM API: For enterprise-grade inference.
  • OpenRouter: For access to diverse open-source model architectures.
  • Tavily Search: Used only when "Web Search" is triggered to provide real-time information.

These providers have their own strict privacy protocols and do not use your inputs to train their base models unless explicitly disclosed by their own policies.

5. Data Security

We implement multi-layered security to protect your data:

  • End-to-End Transit Security: All data is encrypted using industry-standard TLS protocols during transmission.
  • Content Security Policy (CSP): Strict browser policies to prevent XSS and data injection attacks.
  • Sanitization: All AI outputs are sanitized before rendering to ensure your browser remains secure.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. Since most data is stored locally, you can:

  • Delete Your History: Use the sidebar controls to permanently erase local chat records.
  • Opt-Out: Decline the chat history feature to ensure no data is persisted after your session ends.
  • Anonymity: Continue using the service without creating a traditional account, maintaining a purely session-based identity.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact the development team through the project's official GitHub repository.
