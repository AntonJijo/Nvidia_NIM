Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how NVIDIA NIM ("we", "us", and "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our application.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners in order to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

How we use cookies

We use local storage (a technology similar to cookies) solely for the purpose of saving your chat history and preferences on your device. We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or sharing data with third parties.

Cookies We Use

Name Type Purpose
nvidia_nim_cookie_consent Local Storage Stores your preference (accepted/declined) regarding the chat history feature.
nvidia_nim_chat_history Local Storage Stores your past conversations so you can access them later. Containing message content and timestamps.
chatbot_session_id Local Storage Keeps track of your current active session ID to maintain conversation context.

Managing Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or decline the saving of your chat history. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser data or using the "Enable History" / "Decline" options within the application interface.

If you choose to decline, we will not save your chat history, and your conversations will be lost when you refresh or close the page.
